This website serves as a fan-made encyclopedia dedicated to Neon Genesis Evangelion, a groundbreaking Japanese anime series created by Hideaki Anno and produced by the studio Gainax. Originally aired in 1995, the series follows a group of teenagers who pilot giant bio-mechanical robots called Evangelions to protect Earth from mysterious beings known as Angels. With its deep psychological themes, religious symbolism, and complex characters, Evangelion has become one of the most influential and discussed anime of all time. Here, you'll find detailed pages on characters, Eva units, Angels, and media related to the franchise.
